What is PCPair?

PCPair is a student project created to help people choose the best CPUs and GPUs to buy based on real benchmark data.

How are the ranks decided?

PCPair uses 3DMark Timespy overall scores to decide the combined performance of any pair. PCPair takes into consideration the score, price, and value when ranking recommendations for a given price point.

Where is the data from?

Performance data comes from 3DMark Timespy, which is accurate for relative comparisons between pairs, and should also give a decent indication to real DX12 gaming performance. Prices are taken from Canada Computers.
