What is PCPair?

PCPair is a student project created to help people choose the best CPUs and GPUs to buy based on real benchmarks.

How are the ranks decided?

PCPair uses 3DMark Timespy overall scores to decide the combined performance of any pair. Best by value ranks also take into account the score/price ratio to recommend best combinations for a price point.

Where is the data from?

Performance data comes from 3DMark Timespy, a benchmark that should give accurate representations of DX12 gaming performance. Prices are taken from Canada Computers.
